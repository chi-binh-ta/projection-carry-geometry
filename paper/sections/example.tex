\section{Worked Example: 123 Times 321}

In base $10$,
\[
  123 \mapsto (3,2,1), \qquad 321 \mapsto (1,2,3).
\]
The interaction matrix is
\[
\begin{pmatrix}
3 & 6 & 9\\
2 & 4 & 6\\
1 & 2 & 3
\end{pmatrix}.
\]
The anti-diagonal sums are
\[
  c = (3,8,14,8,3).
\]
Carry normalization gives
\[
  d = (3,8,4,9,3),
\]
which reconstructs $39483$.
